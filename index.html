Introduction

CS416 Data Visualizations: Narrative Visualization This dataset is hosted by the City of Austin and contains crimes in the city of Austin dating back to 2003. For this project I decided to look from 2010 onwards.
The visualizations first show some guided charts to help show trends of crime in Austin, Texas. Then the last slide will allow the user to interact and explore the data deeper.
One goal of this visualization is to help the viewer understand crime trends and potentially crime 'hotspots' in Austin, Texas. As a recent migrant to Austin myself, I used this data to help me decide what areas I should look at for purchasing a home.

- Dataset on Austin Government website: hhttps://data.austintexas.gov/Public-Safety/Crime-Reports/fdj4-gpfu
- Provider: Austin Texas Government: https://data.austintexas.gov/
